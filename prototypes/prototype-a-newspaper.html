WE BUILD COMPANIES, NOT PROJECTS.

beatLabs, the Ajman-based digital studio behind 7 independent brands, continues its rapid expansion across apps, agencies, and creative platforms.

In a landscape dominated by freelancers building one-off projects and agencies churning disposable MVPs, beatLabs has taken a fundamentally different path. Each venture under the beatLabs umbrella operates as its own entity — with its own identity, audience, and growth trajectory. The studio doesn't build for clients; it builds for markets.

Founded in March 2026 within Ajman Media City Free Zone, the company has already assembled a portfolio spanning peer-to-peer commerce, subscription management, time tracking, contract automation, web design, wedding photography, and visual arts. Seven modules. Seven brands. One philosophy: build it properly, or don't build it at all.
